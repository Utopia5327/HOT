Design Philosophy

Symbiotic Architecture envisions a future where buildings are living systems — structures that grow, breathe, and evolve alongside nature. Inspired by the intricate habitats of insects and micro-creatures, the project imagines architecture as an extension of natural ecosystems, where apartment towers resemble hollowed trees with inward-facing homes, light-filled atriums, and living facades.

Using AI tools like Midjourney, the series explores what it means for architecture to behave like a living organism. Can structures grow? Can the spaces we inhabit adapt and age like forests? Through iterative, AI-driven image-making, the project blurs the boundary between built form and natural life, proposing a romantic future where architecture is symbiotic, not imposed.
