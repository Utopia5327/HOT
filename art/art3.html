Design Philosophy

In AI × Future Cities, I explore what architecture could become if we stopped seeing it as something separate from nature. What if our buildings could clean the air, grow like plants, and support life rather than just occupy space? This project is not meant to be a literal plan but a question: how can we use artificial intelligence to imagine forms that regenerate the environment instead of depleting it? By collaborating with AI, I want to challenge the idea that technology must always serve profit and speed. Instead, these speculative images suggest that architecture could evolve into something closer to a living organism—a hybrid of human intention and natural processes. It is an invitation to rethink our cities as places where human ambition and ecological care are woven together.
