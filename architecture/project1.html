Ant Studio
This martyrdom memorial project is dedicated to the martyrs of Chhattisgarh state and was commissioned by the Government of Chhattisgarh, India.

Located in Nava Raipur, the emerging capital city of the state, the complex aims to honor the memory of the soldiers and security personnel who sacrificed their lives for the nation, and to inspire future generations with their valor and dedication..
