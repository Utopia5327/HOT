EmotionEcho.exe is an interactive installation that explores the relationship between human emotions and digital environments.

Using real-time emotion detection and generative art, the project creates a dynamic visual and auditory experience that responds to viewers' emotional states, creating a unique feedback loop between human and machine.
about.

EmotionEcho.exe represents a fusion of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and interactive art. The installation uses advanced emotion recognition algorithms to analyze facial expressions and body language in real-time, translating these emotional cues into dynamic visual and auditory responses. Through a combination of generative art techniques and responsive sound design, the system creates an immersive environment that mirrors and amplifies human emotional states. This project explores the potential of technology to create meaningful emotional connections and new forms of human-computer interaction.
