Casa Organica is a proposed residence (G+2 with basement) in Chandigarh city. The ground and first floor are reserved for the client, while the second floor serves as an independent unit for client's in-laws. The basement has servant quarters and a family entertainment hall. The design is a culmination of studying the 5 principals of Le Corbusier, his sculptural brutalist work in Chandigarh and the urban planning of the city.
