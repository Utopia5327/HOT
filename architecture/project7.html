Satoli Residence is a proposed 5-bedroom home designed as an Airbnb retreat for five friends on a hillside site in Uttarakhand. Anchored by a massive natural rock formation, the design responds directly to the site's stepped contours and dense tree cover.
about.

Three distinct volumes are carefully positioned around and atop the rock, with the uppermost cantilevering dramatically over the terrain. The massing is strategically arranged to preserve existing trees—some of which penetrate interior spaces—and to frame unobstructed views of the snow-capped Himalayan range. Circulation flows organically via stairs embedded into the landscape, capitalizing on the site's natural slopes. The lower level accommodates the living room, utilities, and a bedroom. The mid-level contains a bedroom, kitchen opening onto the garden, and a combined lounge and dining area. At the top, perched on the rock, are two bedrooms and an office, opening onto a hilltop terrace featuring an outdoor barbecue and bonfire pit.
